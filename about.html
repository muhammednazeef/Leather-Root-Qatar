About Us

Welcome to Leather Root

Leather Root Trading is a sister concern of Moon Stone Shoes trading, which is been functioning all over the middle east for more than a decade.

Leather Root Trading, established in 2013 operating from Doha, Qatar is well known in the fashion footwear retailers for providing marvelous products. We are sole distributors of Skechers, Woodland, Roadmate etc in Qatar.

As an aim to extend our service and mark our footprints in the development of the country, we have recently set out a division for trading of personal protective equipment (PPE). A well trained and experienced support team of sales executives guarantee to our customers the highest recognized industrial and technical standards.

The several satisfied customers are a practical evidence of our commitment and customer-oriented services. Leather Root Trading is well known among its clients for timely service & uncompromising quality standards. Our Trading Division specializes in safety and Personal Protective Equipment supplies. We are authorized dealers and stockists of leading Safety Brands from across the world. We stock and distribute the products of brands like Volta, Vaultex, Ultima, Armstrong, Prime Safety, etc
